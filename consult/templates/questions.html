Common Questions

How do I know I need therapy?

Therapy is for everyone. The society has lied to us that therapy is for the emotionally dangered but that is not usually the case. Though if you find yourself in situations like depression, persistent family wrangles and excetra, it is highly recommendable to go for therapy.

What if I can't afford?

We are currently working on a financial aid to cater for those unable to afford the fees required.

For how long should I take therapy?

The estimated time by the therapist. When one is undergoing therapy, one ought to be willing to change. This factor is crucial because without the willingness there's no point of the therapy considering high chances of you backsliding are high. But as long as you have a positive attitude towards it and a growth mindset it should be long. After therapy, you will join a support group where you will be helping one another as you grow together.

Does what we talk about in therapy remain confidential?

Yes, off course. We respect your privacy. You took a big step in trusting us and we truly acknowledge that. Our clients' confidentiality is determined by clients themselves. For us, we are just here to help that's all.
